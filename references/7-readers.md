# The 7 Readers Framework

Every piece of content must work for all 7 reader types. Each represents a failure mode to catch.

---

## 1. The Wantrepreneur (2am Scroller)

**Who:** Dreams of buying a business, reads everything, does nothing. 47 browser tabs of Acquire listings. Paralyzed by "what if I pick wrong."

**Kills them:** Abstract advice. "Build relationships." "Be patient."

**Test:** Is there ONE concrete action they could take tomorrow?

---

## 2. The Burned Course-Buyer

**Who:** Spent $2k on a "how to buy a business" course. Joined 3 communities. Still hasn't made an offer. Thinks it's all a scam.

**Kills them:** Guru energy. Polished promises. Vague success stories.

**Test:** Would someone burned trust this? Am I acknowledging what sucks?

---

## 3. The "This Only Works in America" Skeptic

**Who:** In India, SEA, LATAM, or Africa. Reads content, thinks "cool but I can't do this here." No $50K. Doesn't know sellers.

**Kills them:** US-centric examples. Dollar amounts that don't translate.

**Test:** Does this work outside the US? Did I acknowledge different contexts?

---

## 4. The MBA Search Funder

**Who:** HBS/Stanford. Knows ETA theory cold. Has read "HBR Guide to Buying a Small Business." Thinks micro PE is beneath them.

**Kills them:** Anything unsophisticated. But secretly curious about the speed.

**Test:** Something here that challenges their assumptions? Would they learn something new?

---

## 5. The Silent Lurker (3 Months In)

**Who:** Joined Discord/newsletter months ago. Reads everything. Never comments. Has a job they hate. "Next month I'll start looking."

**Kills them:** No urgency. Interesting but not activating.

**Test:** What would make THIS person finally take action?

---

## 6. The "I've Seen This Take" Reader

**Who:** Follows 50 business Twitter accounts. Read every thread. Can predict what you'll say.

**Kills them:** Generic takes. "Buy businesses not start them" (heard it). "Due diligence is important" (duh).

**Test:** Actually a new angle? Would someone who's read everything still find value?

---

## 7. The Skeptical Friend

**Who:** Dev's actual friend who thinks this is either a flex or a scam. Doesn't get why anyone sells a profitable business.

**Kills them:** Anything too good. No failures or downsides. "I made 10x" without "here's the deal that went to zero."

**Test:** Would someone who knows Dev personally believe this? Is it honest?

---

## Review Loop

```
1. Read as each of the 7 readers
   → Mark where each would bounce
   → List what each would hate

2. If ANY reader would bounce:
   → Fix the issues
   → Re-run all 7

3. Only then publish
```
