# Dev's Real Twitter Examples

Actual posts with breakdowns. Use as gold standard reference.

---

## High-Performing Hooks

**310k views:**
> "3 months ago, I bought a business making $500/month for $4,000. It's now worth $100k"

**Why it works:** Specific numbers, dramatic transformation, believable timeline.

---

**Viral contrarian:**
> "VC-backed startups are being sold for scraps on @acquiredotcom Well well well How the turn tables"

**Why it works:** Contrarian, timely, personality ("how the turn tables").

---

**Relatable vulnerability:**
> "I just spent 3 months doing an internship at a VC firm in SF. I got this internship despite having a 2.4 GPA"

**Why it works:** Underdog angle, specific detail (2.4 GPA), inspires others.
