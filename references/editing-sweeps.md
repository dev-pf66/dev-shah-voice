# Dev Voice Editing Sweeps

7 passes to make any content sound like Dev. Run in order.

---

## Sweep 1: Cut the Fluff

**Goal:** Remove every word that doesn't earn its place.

**Kill list:**
- "I think" / "I believe" / "In my opinion"
- "Very" / "Really" / "Extremely"
- "Just" / "Actually" / "Basically"
- "In order to" → "to"
- Any sentence that says what you're about to say

**Test:** Can you cut 20% and lose nothing? Cut it.

---

## Sweep 2: Add Numbers

**Goal:** Every claim needs a number or specific example.

**Before:** "I looked at a lot of deals"
**After:** "I looked at 80 deals"

**Before:** "Made good money"
**After:** "Made $127K"

**Before:** "Took a while"
**After:** "Took 6 months"

**If you can't add a number, cut the claim.**

---

## Sweep 3: Find the Contrarian Angle

**Goal:** Challenge something. Create tension.

**Questions to ask:**
- What does "everyone" say about this that's wrong?
- What's the uncomfortable truth?
- What would surprise people?

**If no contrarian angle exists, find one or don't post.**

---

## Sweep 4: Punch Up the Hook

**Goal:** First 1-2 lines must stop the scroll.

**Test:** Would you click if you saw just the hook?

**Techniques:**
- Lead with the outcome/number
- Create a curiosity gap
- State something surprising
- Use contrast (before → after)

**If the hook doesn't pass, rewrite until it does.**

---

## Sweep 5: Voice Check

**Goal:** Sound like Dev, not like AI.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Short sentences? (No sentence > 20 words)
- [ ] Active voice? ("We did X" not "X was done")
- [ ] Contractions? ("It's" not "It is")
- [ ] Any corporate speak? (Kill it)
- [ ] Would Dev say this out loud?

**Read aloud. If it sounds stiff, rewrite.**

---

## Sweep 6: Add the Real

**Goal:** Include real failures, doubts, or downsides.

**Questions:**
- What went wrong?
- What would you do differently?
- What's the catch?

**If it's all wins, add a loss. If it's all certainty, add a doubt.**

Honesty > polish.

---

## Sweep 7: CTA Check

**Goal:** End with something for the reader to do.

**Options:**
- Question to drive comments
- Ask for DMs/replies
- Direct action ("Follow for more")
- Challenge ("What would you do?")

**Never end flat. Always end with engagement.**

---

## Quick Reference

| Sweep | Question | Fix |
|-------|----------|-----|
| 1. Fluff | Can I cut 20%? | Cut it |
| 2. Numbers | Is there a specific number? | Add one or cut claim |
| 3. Contrarian | Does it challenge something? | Find the tension |
| 4. Hook | Would I click on just this? | Rewrite opener |
| 5. Voice | Would Dev say this out loud? | Make it conversational |
| 6. Real | Is there a failure/downside? | Add honesty |
